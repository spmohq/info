Math Support for Parents: Prompt Suite

A series of prompts to create resources for parents using ChatGPT.

Example: Refining Your Idea

From:

their children are poor in mathematics
parents who are struggling with regarding their children’s education

To:

Target Audience: (Who they are)
Parents whose children struggle with mathematics
Focus Area / Niche: (What you’re exploring)
Helping children improve in math and develop confidence in learning
Research Goal: (Why you’re researching)
To discover what challenges parents face when helping their children learn mathematics, and how ChatGPT or AI tools could support them

Prompt 1: The Prompt Bank

Produce a 50-item ChatGPT prompt bank tailored for parents (Diagnostic Prompt - Finds the weak spot, Story-Explainer Prompt - Builds intuitive understanding, Step-by-Step Solution Prompt - Demonstrates exact process, Practice-Sheet Generator Prompt- Reinforces learning through repetition).

Prompt 2: The Content Calendar

Map a 3-week content calendar you can post into the specific communities above (Facebook posts, Reddit threads, Instagram reels, Quora answers) to start “borrowing those audiences.

Prompt 3: The Posting Kit

I would like you to take this calendar and turn it into a content-ready posting kit — with prewritten captions, sample replies, and a hashtag bank for each platform (so I can copy-paste and go live immediately).

Prompt 4: The Community Launch

I would like you to now create a Bonus Week (Week 4) — with launch content for my own community group (Facebook or Reddit), including my welcome post, engagement rules, and 5 kickoff prompts to get parents talking from day one.

Prompt 5: The Starter Pack PDF

I would like you to design the “Starter Pack PDF” (mentioned above) — a beautifully formatted 4-page printable with 20 ready-to-use ChatGPT prompts + reflection space, so I can upload it as my group’s first pinned resource.

Prompt 6: The Reflection Journal

I would like you to create a fifth page add-on (a “Parent Reflection Journal”) — one printable sheet parents can reuse weekly to track effort, mood, and math progress. It pairs beautifully with this starter pack as a long-term engagement tool for my community.
